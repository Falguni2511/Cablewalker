Our Services

Complete Diagnostics

Video monitoring, magnetic scanning of ground wire, wire core, thermal imaging control, ultraviolet diagnostics, etc. Laser scanning of the security zone of overhead lines, including control of dimensions and woody and shrubby vegetation zone.

Express Diagnostics

Utilize video monitoring and magnetic scanning to ensure integrity of the ground wire.
Laser scanning of the security zone of overhead lines to ensure precise control, including control of woody and shrubby vegetation zone.

Maintenance

Cleaning a wire or ground wire. Coating wires and overhead ground wires protects them from corrosion in the atmosphere or from ice and frost overlay. Installation of special objects on wires and ground wires (signal balls, short circuit indicators, other sensors).

Read More...

Local Repair

Installation of temporary repair clamps (couplings, shunts) on a wire or ground wire to fix the unweaving of the wire or ground wire. Preventing further unweaving of the twine. These clamps are crucial for immediate restoration, and maintaining safety standards on the electrical grid.

Transmission Line Patrolling

Transmission line patrolling with drones is gaining popularity across the energy and utility sectors due to its efficiency and effectiveness in maintaining critical infrastructure. It combines cutting-edge technology with practical solutions to ensure the reliable transmission of electrical power.

Lidar

Using LiDAR (Light Detection and Ranging) technology in power line inspections offers several benefits, enhancing the efficiency and accuracy of the inspection process. LiDAR is a remote sensing method that uses laser pulses to measure distances and create detailed 3D maps of the environment.
